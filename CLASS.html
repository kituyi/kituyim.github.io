OLD GIRL'S ASSOCIATION CLASS OF 2009

JUBILATION OF SUPREME SOLID 1OO YEARS (1922-2022)

St.Agnes catholic girl's boarding primary school naggalama is a catholic girls founded school established by the little sisters of St.Francis in 1922. The vision was started by the late mother superior Mother Kevin who established similar schools around Uganda and East Africa.

The class of 2009 came up with an idea to start an old girl's association to bring close all old girls who have been through this great school and also discuss ideas towards making St.Agnes a better school.

AIMS OF THE ASSOCIATION:

  • discuss matters about school
  • start up projects to benefit both school and old girls

FUTURE PROSPECTS:

  1. create an income generating project
  2. support current students through motivatinal and carrer speeches
  3. support the needy students through donation of scholarstic materials like:
    • books
    • pecncils
    • pens
    • sanitary towels
  4. support best P.L.E candidates with partial startup school fees for secondary school
